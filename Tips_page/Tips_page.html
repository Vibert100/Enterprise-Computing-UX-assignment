Getting started

Going to the gym as a beginner can be confusing for many people. To help guide beginners we recommend you follow one of the most popular workout routines known as the push, pull and legs routine. This workout routine involves doing dedicating a day for push exercises, pull exercises and leg exercises. To help beginners find good exercises for this routine, videos will be provided below.
